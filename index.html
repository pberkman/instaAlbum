Instructions

Spread the love!


instaAlbum is about creating a fun and easy way to share an album with friends and family using Instagram hashtags.

Find a # to post with your photos in order to capture all your memories. instaAlbum will search by that hashtag to compile your album.

Make sure to share the # with all your loved ones too so they can add to the album!
Non Instagram members won't be able to post pictures to the album but will still be able to see all the photos here!

Make sure to find a unique # for your album so you reduce the chances of other photos being added into your album.

How to find an album


The search field will look for the most recent photos that have been "tagged" with the hashtag that you submit. Insert the hashtag for the ablum you are looking for. Photos that have been tagged with your input will appear below.
